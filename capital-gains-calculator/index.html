Frequently Asked Questions

What is the LTCG exemption on equity?

Long-term capital gains on listed equity and equity-oriented mutual funds (where STT is paid) are taxed at 12.5% on gains exceeding ₹1,25,000 per financial year.

How is property LTCG calculated?

For many assets, LTCG is sale price minus cost of acquisition and improvement. Indexation may apply for properties acquired before specified dates — get professional advice.

Can I set off capital losses?

Yes, subject to Income Tax Act rules — STCL can offset STCG/LTCG in prescribed order. Losses may be carried forward.
